# Wormhole PvE Combat Site Intelligence (C1 - C5)

This guide documents the "Sleeper" combat anomalies found in wormhole space. Use this to prepare your tank and prioritize triggers.

## 0. General Rules for All Classes
- **Damage Types:** Sleepers deal **Omni damage** (Equal parts EM, Thermal, Kinetic, and Explosive). Your tank must be uniformly balanced and should not have any "resist holes."
- **Resistances:** Sleepers also have **Omni resists**, meaning you can use any damage type against them without a penalty. However, missiles and drones are popular because they can easily switch to whatever you're best trained in.
- **Triggers:** Killing the "Trigger" ship instantly spawns the next wave. **Never kill the trigger first** unless you want to be overwhelmed.
- **Point/Web:** In C2 and higher, frigates will frequently Warp Scramble or Web you.

## Class 1 (C1)
*Entry-level sites. Can be soloed in Destroyers or Passive Shield Gila/Drake.*

### Typical Site: Perimeter Ambush Point
- **Difficulty:** Easy (~5-10m ISK blue loot).
- **Waves:** 3
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Emergent Defender (Frigate)
    - Wave 2: Emergent Defender (Frigate)
- **Ships:** Algos, Sunesis, or any T1 Cruiser.

---

## Class 2 (C2)
*Step up in difficulty. Ideal for T1 Cruisers or Battlecruisers.*

### Typical Site: Perimeter Checkpoint
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (~15-20m ISK blue loot).
- **Waves:** 4
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Awakened Defender (Cruiser)
    - Wave 2: Awakened Defender (Cruiser)
    - Wave 3: Awakened Defender (Cruiser)
- **Ships:** Gila, Drake, Confessor.

---

## Class 3 (C3)
*The "Sweet Spot" for solo pilots. High profit, manageable risk.*

### Site: Fortification Frontier Stronghold
- **Blue Loot:** ~41m ISK.
- **Special Info:** No Warp Scrambling. Safe to escape if needed.
- **Waves:** 3
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Awakened Defender (Cruiser)
    - Wave 2: Awakened Upholder (Cruiser)
    - Wave 3 (Final): Awakened Preserver (Cruiser) - **Note:** Killing this early despawns the site.
- **Recommended Ships:** Passive Shield Gila, Battle-fit Praxis (very popular for C3).

### Site: Outpost Frontier Stronghold
- **Blue Loot:** ~45m ISK.
- **Special Info:** No Energy Neutralization (Neuts). Best for cap-dependent ships.
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Sleepless Defender (Battleship)
    - Wave 2: Awakened Defender (Cruiser)
- **Recommended Ships:** Gila, Praxis, Myrmidion.

---

## Class 4 (C4)
*Difficult solo due to extreme spawn distances (up to 100km). Better in pairs.*

### Typical Site: Frontier Barracks
- **Blue Loot:** ~80-100m ISK.
- **Waves:** 4
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Awakened Patroller
    - Wave 2: Awakened Patroller
    - Wave 3: Sleepless Sentinel (Battleship)
- **Recommended Ships:** Multi-boxed Gilas, Rattlesnake, Marauder (Vargur/Paladin).

---

## Class 5 (C5)
*End-game PvE. High damage, heavy neuting, massive logistics required.*

### Site: Core Garrison
- **Blue Loot:** ~250m ISK.
- **Special Info:** Features "Capital Escalations." If a Dreadnought/Carrier is on grid, extra waves of Sleepless Guardians spawn.
- **Waves:** 4
- **Triggers:**
    - Wave 1: Awakened Sentinel (Cruiser)
    - Wave 2: Emergent Keeper (Frigate)
    - Wave 3: Sleepless Sentinel (Battleship)
- **Recommended Ships:** 
    - **Solo:** High-end Marauder (Paladin/Vargur/Golem) with heavy capacitor injection.
    - **Group:** Tinker Rattlesnakes (Cap-recharging each other) or Nestors.

### Pro-Tips for Gas Huffers
1. **The "Ninja" Window:** Most gas sites have a **15-20 minute timer** after you warp in before the Sleeper sentries spawn. You can "Ninja" huff the most valuable gas and warp out before they arrive.
2. **Instrumental/Vital:** These sites spawn **Sleepers immediately** (or very quickly) and they are very dangerous (Battleships). Do not enter these in a Prospect/Venture unless the site has been cleared.
3. **C320 is King:** If you find an Instrumental Core Reservoir in a C5/C6, it is often worth more than the combat sites themselves.
