# NPC Null-Sec (The "Wild West with a Saloon")

NPC Null-Sec is a unique tier of space where sovereignty is held by NPC pirate factions rather than player alliances. This creates a "livable" but lawless environment characterized by permanent NPC stations open to all.

## 📍 Key NPC Null-Sec Regions

| Region | Primary Faction | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stain** | Sansha's Nation | High-value Relic sites. Drops Sansha salvages/BPCs (Nightmare/Slave). |
| **Venal** | Guristas Pirates | Caldari pirate heartland. Excellent for Pith/Dread Guristas loot. |
| **Curse** | Angel Cartel | Rugged terrain with many "pipes." Dominant tactical region for travel. |
| **Syndicate** | Intaki Syndicate | Home to mercenaries/independent corps. Good for learning Null-Sec flow. |
| **Outer Ring** | ORE | The gold standard for high-end mining and gas in dangerous space. |
| **Great Wildlands** | Thukker Tribe | Lonely region. **Caution:** No NPC stations; must use player structures. |

## 🎭 The Culture of NPC Null

- **Livable Security:** Unlike player-owned Null-Sec, NPC stations **always allow docking** regardless of your standings. They serve as permanent "Safe Harbors."
- **The Undock Risk:** Because everyone can dock, your enemies often live in the same station. Always assume there is a camp right outside the docking ring.
- **Rules of the Road:** Bubbles are legal. Bombs are legal. You appear in Local immediately upon entering (unlike Wormholes). 
- **The Resident vs. Nomad:** Residents "police" their home pockets and are often elite capital hunters. Nomads (Explorers/Scavengers) slip in and out before being trapped.

## 🍤 Shrimp's Tactical Advice

1.  **Entrance Priority:** **Syndicate** is best for practice (close to K-Space). **Venal** is best for profit (if you can survive the travel).
2.  **Survival:** Always use sub-2.0s align ships (like the specialized **Jackdaw** hauler) to bypass gate-camp bubbles.
3.  **Intel:** Check ZKillboard for the home station before docking to see if it's a known "Kick-out" station or gate-camp hub.
