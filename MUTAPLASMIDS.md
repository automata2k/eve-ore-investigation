# Abyssal Modules & Mutaplasmids (2026 Research)

A guide to the current landscape of mutated modules, market tools, and "Synth" variations as of March 2026.

## Market & Search Tools

Since the retirement of `mutaplasmid.space`, the community has moved to newer platforms for searching and appraising rolled modules.

### 1. MutaMarket
The current gold standard for searching public contracts and managing personal inventory.
* **URL:** [https://mutamarket.com/](https://mutamarket.com/)
* **Features:** Live contract search, stat filtering (CPU/Power/Damage), and AI-driven price prediction.

### 2. Abyss Tracker
Focused on the economic data of Abyssal runs.
* **URL:** [https://abysstracker.com/](https://abysstracker.com/)
* **Usage:** Use this to track the drop rates of specific mutaplasmids and the profitability of different Abyssal tiers.

## Understanding Mutaplasmid Tiers

When "rolling" a module, you choose one of three tiers of mutaplasmids. The higher the tier, the higher the "swing" in potential stats.

| Tier | Name | Potential | Cost | Risk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low** | **Decayed** | Small (-10% to +10% typical) | Very Low | Low |
| **Medium** | **Gravid** | Medium (-20% to +20% typical) | Moderate | Medium |
| **High** | **Unstable** | Massive (-40% to +40% typical) | High | Very High |

## "Synth" Variations

In the context of Abyssal modules, "Synth" is often used loosely to describe the baseline, low-impact versions of mutation.

### Decayed Mutaplasmids (The "Synth" of Rolls)
* Often called "Synth" by players because they represent the baseline, low-impact version of mutation.
* **Best Use:** Fixing a fit that is marginally over CPU/Powergrid limits for a few thousand ISK rather than billions.
