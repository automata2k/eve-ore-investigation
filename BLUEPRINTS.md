## ðŸš€ The "Mission Hub" Strategy (Arbitrage Manufacturing)
The best ROI in High Sec isn't manufacturing for Jita; it's manufacturing **for the people running Level 4 missions**.

### 1. The Play
Identify the ammo and drones used in a specific mission hub.
*   **Example (Osmon):** Missioners there fight Guristas. They need **Kinetic damage** (Scourge Missiles / Hybrid Railgun charges) and **Thermal/Kinetic defense**.
*   **The Blueprint:** Buy the BPOs for these items, research them to **ME 10 / TE 20**, and setup a production line in a nearby Raitaru.

### 2. The Margin Fix
Don't buy your minerals in Jita. Use the **Ore Arbitrage** techniques from the main README. 
*   Mine or buy raw ore in regional pockets.
*   Refine locally to get your Tritanium and Pyerite.
*   Your "Mineral Cost" will be ~10-15% lower than your competitors buying in Jita.
