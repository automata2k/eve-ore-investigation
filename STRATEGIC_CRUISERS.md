# Strategic Cruiser Comparison (T3C Meta 2026)

Strategic Cruisers are defined by their versatility. Subsystem selection allows a single hull to transition between a cloaky scanner, a fleet logistics platform, or a heavy-hitting combatant.

## 🏆 Current Popularity Ranking (Solo/Small-Gang)

| Rank | Ship | Tactical Role | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Loki** | The Swiss Army Knife | Unrivaled versatility. Bonus to **Webifier Range** (30km+) allows perfect engagement control. Equally viable for Shield or Armor tanking. |
| **2** | **Tengu** | The PvE Powerhouse | The gold standard for Class 3 Wormholes and DED 6/10+ sites. Massive **Active Shield Tank** combined with missile damage application. |
| **3** | **Proteus** | The Unseen Assassin | Recent buffs to **Point Range** and **Drone Capacity** have made it a premier hunter. Often used for "Long-Point" tackle from a cloaked unmask. |
| **4** | **Legion** | The Fleet Specialist | Dominates C5/C6 Wormhole fleet brawls. Features superior **Armor Buffer** and mandatory **Energy Neutralizer** pressure for group logistics. |

## 🛰️ Subsystem Philosophies

### Tactical Mobility vs. Security
*   **Interdiction Nullifier:** Now an **Active Module**. It provides temporary immunity to warp bubbles. Vital for traversing Null-sec and J-space pipelines.
*   **Covert Reconfiguration:** Allows the use of **Covert Ops Cloaks** (warping while invisible). This subsystem is required for the "Hunter" archetype.

### Fitting Challenges: The "God Tank" Puzzle
Fitting a **Large Shield Booster (LSB)** or **X-Large** on a Strategic Cruiser (especially the Tengu) is a technical bottleneck.
*   **Powergrid Constraints:** Standard combat subsystems prioritize damage. Fitting a Battleship-sized booster usually requires the **Electronic Efficiency Gate** (Core) or specific **Abyssal modules**.
*   **Implants:** High-end "Gristum" fits often utilize **Zainou 'Gypsy'** implants to bridge the powergrid gap. Without implants, a "True Buffer" (LSE-based) or "Medium Active" tank is more stable.

*   **For Wealth Generation:** Prioritize the **Tengu**.
*   **For Independent Roaming:** Prioritize the **Loki**.
*   **For Ambush Tactics:** Prioritize the **Proteus**.
*   **For Heavy Fleet Combat:** Prioritize the **Legion**.
